EMPLOYMENT RECRUITMENT AGREEMENT

Between Employer and Consultancy

This Employment Recruitment Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of {{ current_date }} by and between:

{{ employer.company_name }}, a company having its principal place of business at {{ employer.address }} ("Employer"),

And

{{ consultancy.name }}, a recruitment consultancy having its principal place of business at {{ consultancy.address }} ("Consultancy").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Employer has posted a job opening for {{ job.title }} on the Zyukthi platform;

WHEREAS, Consultancy has submitted a successful bid to assist in filling this position with qualified candidates;

WHEREAS, Employer wishes to engage Consultancy's services to identify and refer qualified candidates for the Position;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. ENGAGEMENT

Employer hereby engages Consultancy to provide recruitment services for the Position of {{ job.title }} with the following details:

  • Job Type: {{ job.get_job_type_display }}
  • Experience Level: {{ job.get_experience_level_display }}
  • Work Mode: {{ job.get_work_mode_display }}
  • Location: {{ job.location }}
  • Salary Range: {{ job.min_salary }} to {{ job.max_salary }} {{ job.currency }} ({{ job.get_salary_type_display }})
  • Vacancies: {{ job.vacancies }}
2. CONSULTANCY SERVICES

Consultancy agrees to:

  • Use reasonable efforts to identify and refer qualified candidates for the Position within {{ job.deadline|default:"the agreed timeline" }}.
  • Conduct preliminary screening of candidates to ensure they meet the minimum qualifications specified by Employer.
  • Provide Employer with complete and accurate information about each candidate referred.
  • Not refer any candidate who has already been submitted by another consultancy or directly applied to Employer for the same Position.
3. FEES AND PAYMENT

In consideration for the services provided by Consultancy, Employer agrees to pay Consultancy a fee of {{ bid.fee }} {{ job.currency }} as per the accepted bid proposal.

The fee shall be payable according to the following schedule:

  • 50% upon signing of this Agreement
  • 50% upon successful placement of a candidate and completion of the probation period

All payments shall be made within 15 days of invoice date.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until:

  • The Position is filled and the probation period is completed; or
  • The Position is withdrawn by Employer; or
  • Either party terminates this Agreement with 14 days written notice.
5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all candidate information, business processes, and any proprietary information shared during the course of this engagement.

6. GENERAL PROVISIONS
  • This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.
  • Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
  • This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
